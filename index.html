~ Who we are ~

Bodiredi Maranyane

Bodiredi Maranyane is an enterprise with a focus on utilization of renewable energy for off grid applications. The off grid applications include street lighting, domestic households, education centres, boreholes, factories, health centres, etc.

Bodiredi Maranyane Objectives

The objectives of Bodiredi Maranyane are to contribute to universal access to electricity using renewable energy as a bridging technology for the majority of the poor who cannot afford to pay cost covering prices. In addition, Bodiredi Maranyane aims to contribute towards reducing carbon emissions by promoting renewable (cleaner) technologies for self- sustainability
Bodiredi Maranyane is registered with Company Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC), registration number: 2019 / 265843 / 07 and is compliant.


Vision

Our vision is sustainable development through utilization of renewable energy to conserve the environment and increase access to reliable and affordable electricity for self-sustainability.

Mission

Our mission is to contribute to universal access to clean, reliable and affordable electricity through the design and provision of customized solutions, efficient and quality service as well as adherence to the highest standards of safety and compliance.
